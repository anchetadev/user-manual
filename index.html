Everything you need to know about working with me. Couldn't find an answer to a question you have? No worries, shoot me a message or email!

Common Questions

Who are you???

Sr Instructional Designer

AKA Chaos Coordinator

I basically build, map, and manage instructor trainings for boot camps.

One Pet Peeve

Littering

Do NOT litter in my presence!! No further explanation!!

What’s the best way to convince you of something?

Different perspectives - If you can shine a light on something in a new way, I’ll definitely rethink my own position.

What are some honest, unfiltered things about you?

I value your time outside of work more than I value the time you spend at work. As long as we are meeting our expectations and working effectively and efficiently, I don’t care if you leave 5 seconds after you finish everything that was expected from you for the day.

How to get a Gold Star from me

I give gold stars to people who aren’t afraid to challenge authority, a traditional way of doing things, or rules. Sometimes it takes bravery to make something better and I’m always looking for better and more efficient. Also people who aren’t judgemental and can see many different sides to things.

Communication

In an action oriented way - I don’t do well with sole criticism. I need to know what needs to change. That being said, I'll often lead with what I think needs to change. I won't criticize something unless I can think of what I would do to change it so sometimes I need to be prompted for feedback else I may fester in my own head busy perfecting them even when I want to share them.

Written. I have to often manage various contexts for multiple projects throughout the week. This combined with my awful memory leaves me at a high risk of forgetting, misinterpreting, and/or misremembering things. It is frustrating for me too! I need important conversations to be documented and requests via written format.

Directly. Tell me what you need from me first, reasoning later. If you need help making a decision, same thing - tell me the outcome/action first and then explain the reasoning behind it. I can process a lot of different perspectives and I will play devil’s advocate, but only to the benefit of the outcome. I never take a different perspective to judge or belittle another person’s position. Sometimes I can get annoyed if I feel like someone is making me think for them, but with action leading the conversation, I understand that you’ve given lots of thought to whatever you’re asking me which makes me feel like you value my time.

Work

I feel most energized when I find a state of flow, where the absence of tedium, a focus on high-impact tasks, and an absence of ambiguity come together to create an optimized work experience. Big energy boost is when I know I can finish and leave early!

Tedious activities. I totally can do it and push through, but you'll probably catch me trying to automate at least one part of whatever tedious process I'm working with.

As a person with a mile wide, inch deep approach to life, there are few things I wouldn't want to do. Thematically, the tasks I have enjoyed all share a few things in common: I love tasks that require technical and/or non-technical problem solving, novelty, and generate high impact.

Interpersonal

I value diligence, curiosity, authenticity, and integrity. Stubbornness is welcome around me as someone who is also strong-minded! I like people who aren’t afraid of being challenged.

My coaching style is very laissez-faire. If you want to make progress, you need to let me know directly how I can support you. Otherwise, I will assume that you’ve got it. I can make assumptions about what will help you best, but I will not prefer making them.

As an extremely action oriented person and someone passionate about efficiency, many folks may make the assumption that I am extremely tied to results and hitting benchmarks. While I can be intense in my pursuit of efficiency and excellence, I give warm welcome to trial and error and making mistakes. Some may also find that I care too much about responsibility, however, I understand that my fellow workers are human too and we have so much more to us than our lives at work. Know that my core motivation is that I believe the faster we can make something and make it well, the faster we can go home (and the better we can make a piece of the world)!

Thank you for reading!
